Tank Screens and Solar Shields

Maintain the quality of water in your tank by preventing the ingress of leaves, sunlight, debris and pests.

Available in a range of sizes, our tank screens and solar shields prevent leaves, debris, mosquitoes, vermin and sunlight from entering your rainwater tank via the tank inlet.
